Pizza

Description

Today, we are going to make a simple home-made pizza.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (250g)
  • 3/4 cup warm water (180g)
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the toppings:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or marinara
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings: sliced mushrooms, pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, onions, basil, etc.

Steps

  1. Make the dough:
    • In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix with a spoon or hands until a dough forms.
    • Knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth and stretchy. Add a little more flour if too sticky.
    • Cover with a cloth and let it rise for 45–60 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone or baking sheet, put it in the oven while it heats.
  3. Shape the dough:
    • On a floured surface, roll or stretch the dough into a circle or rectangle about ¼ inch thick.
  4. Add toppings:
    • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough
    • Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake:
    • Place the pizza on a hot baking sheet or stone.
    • Bake for 10–12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. 6. Serve:
    • Let it cool for 1–2 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
