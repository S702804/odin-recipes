Ice-cream

Description

Today, we are going to create a simple vanilla ice-cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream (cold)
  • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt (optional, enhances flavor)

Steps

  1. Whip the cream:
    • In a large bowl, whip the cold heavy cream using a hand mixer or stand mixer until stiff peaks form (about 2–3 minutes).
  2. Add the rest:
    • In a separate bowl, mix the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt.
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the condensed milk mixture using a spatula until fully combined. Be careful not to deflate the cream too much.
  3. Freeze:
    • Pour the mixture into a loaf pan or airtight container.
    • Cover with plastic wrap or a lid.
    • Freeze for at least 6 hours, or overnight, until firm.
  4. Serve
    • Scoop and enjoy like regular ice cream!
